Hera — Final Test-net before main-net launch.

The Hera test-net is now live https://hera.juno.aneka.io/validators

KaranSingh Gill in Life Rest

Purpose of Hera is to run the custom inflation module and test before mainnet launch on October 1st.

We ask all validators to please join Hera right now!

✅ How to join a testnet on Juno?
✅ Take me to Hera.

Please continue to run “Lucina” the previous test-net so that developers can deploy applications for JunoHack and test them. Lucina has CosmWasm enabled and Hera does not.

Delegators that qualify for the Feb 18th snapshot can now delegate their test-Juno on Hera via the http://juno.OmniFLix.co

Simply connect to Keplr and choose either Lucina or Hera testnets and sign a tx from your stakedrop acct. Your test-Juno will appear and you can delegate your stake and experience how mainnet rewards will be.

Note: Test-net rewards do not carry over to mainnet.

For additional questions or information please join the Juno Discord or Telegram chat.
